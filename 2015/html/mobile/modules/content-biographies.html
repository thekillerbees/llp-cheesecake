Chef Bok Okura
Vice President, Research & Development: Bakery
Chef Bob Okura, our award-winning Corporate Executive Chef, and his culinary team are responsible for creating and perfecting the dishes that are featured on our menu. Chef Bob has been with The Cheesecake Factory for over 20 years, creating hundreds of the dishes that are featured on our menu today. From our new Small Plates and Snacks to our classic Miso Salmon, if you have a favorite Cheesecake Factory dish, chances are Chef Bob had his hand in creating it.
