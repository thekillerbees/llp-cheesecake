Welcome to The Cheesecake Factory

The Cheesecake Factory menu features more than 200 menu selections made fresh from scratch each day – using only the highest quality ingredients – which combines to create our remarkable tastes.

We will gladly honor requests to modify your order to suit specific health or dietary needs. The Cheesecake Factory is famous for our generous portions which are ideal for sharing or taking home for a second meal – but be sure to save room to enjoy one of 50 legendary cheesecakes and specialty desserts.

You Can Have Your Cake... And Send It Too!

A selection of our most popular Cheesecakes, as well as our Gift Cards, can be shipped almost anywhere in the U.S. via Federal Express! Shop Online or call 1-800-330-CAKE - 24 hours, 7 days a week.

Made with only the finest ingredients, every Cheesecake is lovingly hand-packaged and delivered to your door by Federal Express. The Cheesecake Factory 10" Cheesecakes arrive pre-sliced and serve 12-16; our 7" Cheesecakes are whole and serve 6-8.

The Cheesecake Factory gift cards make the perfect gift for any taste. They can be used for shopping online, by telephone or at any Cheesecake Factory restaurant.
